The 7 Core Systems

Functional medicine assesses interconnected physiological systems to identify root causes of health imbalances, rather than treating isolated symptoms.

Assimilation & Gut

Digestion, absorption, microbiome balance, and intestinal barrier integrity form the foundation of nutrient delivery.

Defense & Repair

Immune function and inflammatory response work together to protect, heal, and maintain cellular integrity.

Energy

Mitochondrial function drives cellular energy production, influencing vitality, metabolism, and overall performance.

Biotransformation & Elimination

Detoxification pathways process and eliminate toxins, supporting liver, kidney, and cellular waste management.

Communication

Hormonal and neurotransmitter signaling coordinate every bodily function from mood to metabolism.

Transport

Cardiovascular and circulatory systems deliver oxygen, nutrients, and immune factors throughout the body.

Structural Integrity

Musculoskeletal health and barrier systems maintain physical structure, mobility, and protective boundaries.
